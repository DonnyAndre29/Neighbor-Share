Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does Neighbor Share ensure the safety and security of transactions?

    Neighbor Share prioritizes safety by allowing users to communicate through the platform's messaging system. It's recommended to arrange meetings in public places and adhere to community guidelines. Additionally, user profiles and reviews contribute to building a trustworthy neighborhood network.

  • What if an item is damaged during the borrowing period?

    In the unfortunate event of damage, communicate with the neighbor through the platform's messaging system to discuss the situation. Neighbor Share encourages open communication and collaborative problem-solving. If a resolution cannot be reached, refer to the community guidelines for guidance.
